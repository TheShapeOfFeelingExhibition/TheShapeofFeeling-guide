Alisha Smith

‘The Colourist’

Alisha Smith, known as The Colourist, is a self-taught Barbadian artist and creative entrepreneur, specializing in realistic portraiture, expressive depictions of local landscapes and more recently, still lifes.

Arianna Aleah

Abstract Realism

Arianna Aleah Holligan is a 22 year-old Barbadian artist defined by Abstract Realism. Deeply inspired by femininity and the natural world, her paintings reflect personal and cultural narratives of quiet strength.
